Finance tracker app

This is a finance tracker app that I made using React and IndexedDB. It allows you to keep track of your expenses and income. I made this app because my girlfriend wanted to keep track of her expenses and income, but she didn't find any app that she liked. So I decided to make one for her. I wanted to make the app completely useful offline as well, that's why I chose to use IndexedDB to store the data. I used IndexedDB to store the data because I learned a little about it at school and wanted to learn how to use it better. I also used React because it makes it easier to make a web app that my girlfriend can use on her phone. I will add more things to the app later on, like a graph that shows the evolution of your expenses and income. But for now the app is finished and my girlfriend is using it to keep track of her expenses and income.
