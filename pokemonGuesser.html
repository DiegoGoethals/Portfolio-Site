Who's that Pokémon online game

This is the first real 'project' I made and wanted to share with you. I made this game because I wanted to improve and enhance my front-end skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The idea for this game actually started when a friend of mine made his own guessing game about Star Wars, you can find this game here on: swrdle.be. After I saw his really well-made game I immediately got the idea to make a similar game about Pokémon. I have always been a fan of Pokémon and I remembered they once had the game during the show to guess which pokémon was displayed.
